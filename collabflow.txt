flowchart TD
    Start([Owner Creates Campaign]) --> Campaign[Campaign Created<br/>Goals, Budget, KPIs]
    
    Campaign --> Discovery{Discovery Phase}
    
    Discovery -->|Path A| BrowseInfluencers[Owner Browses<br/>Influencer Profiles]
    Discovery -->|Path B| BrowseListings[Owner Browses<br/>Service Listings]
    Discovery -->|Path C| ServiceRequest[Influencer Browses<br/>Service Requests]
    
    BrowseInfluencers --> CollabReq[Send Collaboration Request<br/>Status: pending]
    
    BrowseListings --> Offer[Owner Makes Offer<br/>on Service Listing]
    Offer -->|Accepted| CollabReq
    
    ServiceRequest --> Proposal[Influencer Submits Proposal]
    Proposal -->|Accepted| CollabReq
    
    CollabReq -->|Accepted| Contract[Collaboration Contract<br/>Budget & Dates<br/>Status: active]
    CollabReq -->|Rejected| End1([End])
    
    Contract --> Collab[Collaboration Created<br/>Status: active]
    
    Collab --> Board[Collaboration Boards]
    Collab --> Chat[Chat Room Created]
    
    Board --> Tasks[Tasks Assigned<br/>todo to completed]
    
    Chat --> Messages[Messages Exchanged]
    
    Tasks --> Content[Content Execution<br/>Per Calendar]
    
    Content --> Complete{All Tasks Done?}
    Complete -->|Yes| ContractComplete[Contract: completed]
    Complete -->|No| Tasks
    
    ContractComplete --> CollabComplete[Collaboration: completed]
    CollabComplete --> End2([End])
